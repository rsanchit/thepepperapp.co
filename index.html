How do I delete my account?

To close your profile permanently:

  1. Sign into into the Pepper app
  2. Tap your Photo Icon on the far right of the navigation bar
  3. Tap on two dots ".." beside edit profile.
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm

Simply removing, uninstalling or deleting the Pepper app from your phone will not close your account entirely. To close your profile, you must follow the above steps. 

 

This action ensures all your content including photos, chat history, and member interactions will be deleted.

 

Please note:  Removing or uninstalling the app from your phone does not delete your account. You must follow the steps above to delete your account.

 

If you subscribed to Pepper using your Apple ID or Google Play Store ID, deleting your account does not cancel your subscription. You will need to take the necessary steps with the App Store or Google Play Store to ensure you are not charged an auto renewal subscription fee.
